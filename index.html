about us

Adaptive Learning is an interactive video-based platform focused on rendering academic concepts with hands-on and result oriented approaches.Adaptive learning is an online initiative to bring mainstream education to the masses and emancipate them with the understanding of the current happenings and the causes that matter at the same time with the help of technology. We Believe the future is technology-driven and hence education has to keep pace with the dynamic tech world. With our philosophy of Three E’s and commitment to the cause of education, We aim to ENGAGE, EDUCATE AND ENLIGHTEN students holistically in a non-disruptive and cordial online environment. We aim to blend traditional teaching keeping pace with the changing world bringing to you, the best of both worlds.
